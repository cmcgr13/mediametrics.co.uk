Our story

Built from the inside out

Media Metrics was founded by Conor McGregor, a performance marketer with over a decade of experience managing paid media and demand generation programmes for B2B technology companies.

Having spent years running campaigns in-house — managing multi-product portfolios across Google, Microsoft, and LinkedIn Ads — Conor built Media Metrics to give B2B companies access to that same level of strategic depth without the overhead of a full-time hire.

We’re based in the UK and work with clients nationwide.

Who we are

Performance marketing that speaks your language

Media Metrics is a B2B performance marketing consultancy built on a simple premise: ad spend should generate pipeline, not just traffic.

We work with SaaS, technology, and complex-sale businesses to plan, execute, and optimise paid media programmes across search, social, and programmatic channels. Every campaign is structured around commercial outcomes — cost per lead, cost per opportunity, and return on ad spend.

Founded by a marketer who’s managed multi-product portfolios in-house, we bring the strategic depth of an internal team without the overhead.

No vanity metrics. No black-box reporting. Just clear data and honest recommendations.

Who we work with

Built for B2B

We work best with companies that have a considered purchase cycle — typically SaaS, technology, professional services, or complex industrial products. Our sweet spot is businesses spending £10k–£50k per month on paid media that want better returns without building a full in-house team.

If you’re a B2C brand, an e-commerce shop, or looking for someone to “run some ads” without caring about downstream metrics — we’re probably not the right fit. And that’s fine.
